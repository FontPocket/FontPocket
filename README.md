# FontPocket

FontPocket is an alternative way of exploring Google Fonts, while also helping you keep track of your favourite font families.

## A different way to explore Google Fonts

It focuses on the technical properties of font families — making it easier to preview, filter, and compare fonts based on characteristics such as weight availability, width availability, italic support, character subsets, and variable font capabilities.

Whether you’re looking for a variable font with width support, a typeface with extensive weight options, or a font that covers a specific character subset, FontPocket provides a focused workflow for discovering and comparing fonts based on what they can actually do.

## Save your favourite font families

When you find a font worth revisiting, add it to your favourites. Favourites are stored directly in your browser — no account needed.

## Built for designers, developers, and typography enthusiasts

FontPocket is built for designers, developers, and typography enthusiasts who want a different way to explore the Google Fonts library.
